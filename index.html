About Me

I’m a creative artist who blends visual art with makeup and styling to craft expressive, camera-ready looks. My journey began with music videos where I discovered a passion for transforming concepts into striking visuals. Over time, I’ve worked on multiple projects, combining makeup, styling and artistic vision to create cohesive, memorable looks that enhance the overall storytelling. Every project I take on is an opportunity to explore creativity, push boundaries, and bring unique ideas to life on screen.
